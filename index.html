ARISE Around the World

ARISE was on the move in 2025. In Australia, we discipled all ages, helping students from all over Europe discover the meaning and mission in the story of God's love. The program in Finland sparked a new generation of enthusiastic and gospel-centered young adults to light Europe with the truth of Jesus. Our online program reached homes worldwide, and our intensives in Tarragona, Spain, and Houston, Texas, catalyzed communities with gospel clarity.

ARISE is more than a school, course, or project. ARISE is a movement, and Jesus is its champion.

ARISE Australia:

  • 31 students representing six countries: Norway, Switzerland, Austria, New Zealand, the USA, and Australia
  • 86% of the student body was under 30 years old
  • 24 students stayed on for the six-month Bibleworking program, ARISE for Life

ARISE Finland:

  • 32 students representing 10 countries: Finland, Norway, Australia, USA, Canada, Czechia, Switzerland, Kenya, Estonia, and the Netherlands
  • 96% of the student body was under 30 years old

Dwell: The Sanctuary and the Heart of God

For our 2025 Light Bearers Convocation, we gathered in Jasper, Oregon, for four days of worship, fellowship, and studying the sanctuary message. It wasn't just four days of sermons, though. Between the morning walks, evening afterglows, special feature Convo-sations, and engaging kids programs, the Holy Spirit was moving. Commitments to Jesus were made, friendships were renewed, and all of us saw more clearly a God who wants to dwell with us.

■ 10 sessions covering the sanctuary message

■ Hundreds gathered, with attendance peaking on saturday at close to a thousand attendees

■ 8 baptisms

The Work Isn't Done

On behalf of the entire team at Light Bearers, I want to again say thank you for helping us spread the good news of the life, death, and resurrection of Jesus around the world. In a time when you have likely struggled with cynicism, grief, defeat, anger, and a million other emotions, you also chose to hope.

Your contributions helped bring that hope and faith to others. I write with no hyperbole that every tract handed out, every Bible shared, every disciple trained was possible because of you. Each dollar you gave helped reveal to people from Kansas to Kenya the opulent grace and mercy of God, which is the ultimate story this world needs to hear. Thank you.

But the mission isn't over yet.

As you read these words, church planters are trekking through steaming jungles to reach the indigenous tribes in Central America. They need more literature. Young adults are searching for meaning and confidence in their faith. They need to be discipled. Countless people wounded by religion may never step foot in a church again. They need to encounter the gospel where they are, often online. Your support keeps our mission in motion.

As we enter our fortieth year of ministry, we celebrate all that God has done through our partnership and look ahead with renewed determination to keep sharing the unadulterated gospel together.

As we close out 2025, would you consider making a special year-end gift? Your donation will not only strengthen our financial foundation as we wrap up this year but also position us to fund our initiatives in 2026.

Warmly,
Ty Gibson
